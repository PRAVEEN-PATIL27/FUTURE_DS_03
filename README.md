# 📊 Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer behavior across different stages of the marketing funnel using **Microsoft SQL Server** and **Power BI**. The objective is to evaluate customer engagement, measure conversion performance, identify drop-off points, and generate actionable business insights that can improve marketing strategies and increase revenue.

The analysis examines how customers progress through the **Browse**, **Add to Cart**, **Checkout**, and **Purchase** stages while evaluating the impact of marketing channels, regions, devices, and product categories on business performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer interactions across the marketing funnel.
* Measure the overall conversion rate.
* Evaluate total revenue generated.
* Identify customer drop-off points.
* Analyze marketing channel performance.
* Compare regional and device-wise customer behavior.
* Evaluate product category performance.
* Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
* Generate data-driven business insights.
